This is a web application for searching match history in League of Legends.

You can simply type a Summoner name(username) in League of Legends.

You would have a list of most recent 20 matches.

You can mouse over images to read descriptions and name of items or champions and you can also click one of the list to see bit more details.

You can search other users as well by either clicking one of usernames in your match history or entering username.

The web application searches any lastest 20 matches within 6 months. You won't see match history that you have played 6 months ago.

The application supports mobile version and other browsers as well. You can free to use any devices.
